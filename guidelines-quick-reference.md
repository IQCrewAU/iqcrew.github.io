## Fonts (both free)

- **General Sans** (Medium 500 / Semibold 600 / Bold 700) — headings, body, the wordmark. Download free from Fontshare (see FONTS.md).
- **JetBrains Mono** (Regular / Medium) — figures, labels, evidence lines, timestamps. Files included in `fonts/jetbrains-mono/` (SIL OFL).
- Never substitute. Not Inter, not Arial, not Helvetica. If General Sans is unavailable in a tool, use the logo image files — they're outlined and need no fonts.

## Logo rules

- Clearspace: the height of the monogram's "i" on all sides. Minimum sizes: monogram 24 px, full lockup 90 px wide.
- Colourways: primary (light backgrounds) · reverse (dark/green) · mono-ink · mono-paper. Nothing else — no recolouring, gradients, shadows, outlines, rotation, or placing on photography.
- The monogram is the avatar; the horizontal lockup is the default everywhere else; stacked only where the slot is square-ish.
- **The stamp** ("ON THE RECORD", Record Red, tilted −6° to −12°, never straight) appears only on verified artefacts: Scorecard reports, assessments, published case studies, the Trust Centre. Anywhere else is a violation.

## Words we ban — explicit list, no exceptions

solutions · synergy · cyber (as a standalone noun) · world-class · cutting-edge · best-in-class · seamless · unlock · empower · leverage · peace of mind · military-grade · **guaranteed** · revolutionary · game-changing · "trusted by thousands" or any unverifiable social proof

Also banned in all public material: emoji, exclamation marks, Title Case Headings, ALL-CAPS body text, fear-based framing of any kind, and the word "sovereign" outside the Trust Centre and technical pages.

## Words we own

control · visibility · hardened · standards · evidence · on the record · dedicated · properly run

## Voice, in five sliders

Just conversational of centre · plain English with technical receipts one click deep · warm side of serious · understatement over bold claims · enablement-led (~70/30 — name the risk once, then capability).

**We are:** rigorous, straight-talking, calm, warm, evidence-led, Australian.
**We are never:** pedantic, salesy, alarmist, matey, boastful, flag-waving.

Say the mechanism, not the vibe: "MFA on every account" beats "we take security seriously" every single time.

## Taglines (ratified set — use the right line for the right slot)

- **Extend your team, not your risk.** — master, everywhere
- Offshore capability. Onshore control. — decks, one-pagers
- Your people offshore. Your data onshore. — Trust Centre, ads
- Grow the crew. Keep the controls. — LinkedIn, social
- The safe way to staff offshore. — referral partners, plain-English intros
- More capability, not more exposure. — email subject lines
- Your offshore team, properly run. — how-it-works contexts
- Offshore staff, on the record. — monitoring contexts, pairs with the stamp
- Every seat secured. — pricing contexts
- Built like it's audited. Because it is. — Trust Centre
- Global team. Australian standards. — about/brand contexts
- Add people, not attack surface. — technical audiences

## The two rules above all others

1. **Every public claim must exist in the claims register at PRINT NOW status.** No register row, no sentence — however good it sounds.
2. **Australian English, always.** Organise, colour, licence (noun), analyse. Sentence case. Real punctuation.

Questions or judgement calls → check `brand-book.html` §05 (voice) and §08 (doctrine) → then ask Kyle or Justyn. When genuinely unsure, understate.
